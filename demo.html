📞 AI Receptionist + Missed Call Text-Back 4 min

How To Explain It

The core idea: When a call goes unanswered, instead of going to voicemail (which most customers ignore), an AI receptionist picks up, has a real conversation, captures the lead, and texts the owner the full info instantly. Owner calls back already knowing who it is and what they want.

Your Pitch

"So right now, every time you miss a call while you're under someone's unit, that lead is probably calling the next guy on Google. What Torevez does is the second a call goes missed, an AI receptionist jumps on, engages them, captures the lead, and fires it straight into your dashboard with their info. I'll show you live right now — call this number and just let it ring through."

Live Demo

Give them your forwarding-enabled number to call. Let it ring through. Vapi picks up, you let it run a normal flow with you playing customer if needed, or have them play the customer.
When call ends, show them: (1) the office SMS that hit your phone with full lead details, (2) the lead in the dashboard's Leads tab, (3) the lead also in the Calls tab.

3 Closing Questions

  1. "Does that solve the problem you described?"
  2. "What would the impact be if that was running every time you missed a call?"
  3. "Is that something you can see working for your business?"
SILENCE IS YOUR FRIEND. DON'T FILL IT.

📅 Booking Automation + Smart Reminders 3 min

How To Explain It

The core idea: Once a lead's in, they can book directly from a custom page — no phone tag. Automatic confirmation goes out. 24 hours before, they get a reminder. 2 hours before, another reminder. Cuts no-shows by roughly 60%.

Why it matters: Every no-show = wasted truck roll = real money. Labor shortages make every hour of a tech's day expensive.

Your Pitch

"Once that lead comes in, instead of phone tag, they can book directly from a custom booking page built for your business. Automatic confirmations go out immediately. Then 24 hours before the appointment and 2 hours before, they get a reminder text. That alone cuts no-shows by about 60%. Every wasted truck roll to a no-show is money out of your pocket."

Live Demo

💰 Finance Tracker 3 min

How To Explain It

The core idea: A quick P&L tracker for every job. Add revenue when you finish, add costs for parts and labor, see your profit and margin in real time. Export to CSV monthly for your books.

Why it matters right now: Equipment costs are running $9-16K per unit because of the R-410A transition. Margins are getting squeezed. Knowing exactly what each job actually makes is critical.

Your Pitch

"With equipment costs going up and the refrigerant transition squeezing margins, knowing exactly what every job actually makes you matters more than ever. Torevez has a built-in finance tracker — you log the revenue, you log the parts and labor, and it tells you your real profit and margin on every single job. No spreadsheets, no guessing. Let me show you."
